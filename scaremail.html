ScareMail.tk

From Screamer Fanon Wiki, the free encyclopedia of jumpscares

ScareMail.tk was a classic prank site where you could basically mail someone a jump-scare. It’s totally dead now because Freenom (the guys who did .tk domains) got into a massive legal mess with Meta, taking a ton of sites down with them.

The site was pretty simple: you’d put in an email, pick a "scare" from a drop-down menu, and it would show the content. It was famous for having a really weird, random collection of scares that you couldn't find on other sites like PlayScaryGames.

The Scare Selector Library

1. Red Pancake (ScareMail Variant)

Way different from the one on PlayScaryGames. This was a messy collage of Regan MacNeil (The Exorcist) images in a bunch of weird color filters. In the middle, there was a picture of actual red pancakes with the text: "Red, yellow, green....PANCAKES???"

2. Cryman

A creepy gif of a guy in a mask who looks like he's crying. After a few seconds, he suddenly headbutts the camera.

3. Magic Eye

Starts as a video of a stereogram (one of those hidden image things). It then cuts into a montage of screamers from the SCP site using the scream from "What's Wrong with this picture?".

4. Not a screamer

A total troll option. It’s just a plain white screen. No sound, no image, absolutely nothing happens. A lot of people got more scared waiting for the jump than they did with the actual screamers.

5. Screamer for blind

This one is just audio. It starts with a Text-to-Speech voice reading the Wikipedia page for Christmas, then suddenly cuts to the legendary K-Fee car commercial scream.

6. No Pancake

Exactly the same as the ScareMail Red Pancake variant, but the central image of the pancakes is missing.

7. YTMND

Basically a collection of links to old YTMND sites like cutecats1.ytmnd.com and winterrowd.ytmnd.com.

8. K-Fee GIF

A fast-paced montage of all the different K-Fee coffee commercials. Lots of gargoyles and banshees.

9. We are good boys, right cuz' we were here?

A gif showing a collage of "liminal space" photos. It eventually cuts to the Freddy Fazbear scream from FNAF 1.

10. YANI

A "safe" mirror of the infamous You Are An Idiot virus. It has the flashing colors and the song, but none of the scripts that spawn a million windows or lag your PC.

Trivia

  • The site's closure in 2018 marked the end of an era for .tk prank sites.
  • "Screamer for blind" is often cited as the most creative (and annoying) audio-only prank of its time.
  • The YTMND links are mostly dead now since YTMND went through its own shutdown/archive phase.

Rest in peace ScareMail.tk (Unknown - 2018)
