Red Pancake

From Screamer Fanon Wiki, the free encyclopedia of jumpscares

Red Pancake is a weird one. Unlike most of the stuff on PlayScaryGames.fun, this isn't a game and it doesn't even have a scream. It's just a straight-up shock page that people use to gross each other out.

The Page Content

When you land on the page, the first thing you see is some simple text at the top that says "Wow, you did it". Right under that text is the main "attraction"—a really nasty photoshopped image of a guy.

The edit makes the person look like he's decomposing. The skin is all gray and rotted, and the most disturbing part is that he has no eyes—just empty, dark sockets. It's obviously fake if you look close, but it's enough to mess with you if you aren't expecting it.

The "redpancake.org" Mystery

There’s a watermark on the image that says redpancake.org. If you actually try to go to that URL, it doesn't lead to a separate site. It's just a masked redirect that sends you straight back to the playscarygames.fun/redpancake directory. It's basically just a trick to make the "brand" look more official than it actually is.

Trivia

  • Even though there's no scream, it's still banned for younger users because the image is pretty graphic.
  • Some people call it a "silent screamer" because of the visual shock factor.
  • The "Red Pancake" name doesn't really have anything to do with the image, which is common for these types of random shock sites.
  • A lot of people accidentally find this while looking for actual pancake recipes, which is probably the point.
